Self Nurture

Self-nurture refers to the practices, habits, and activities that individuals engage in to care for their emotional, mental, and sometimes physical well-being

Life can be overwhelming, and we all need support sometimes. That's why we created Self Nurture‚Äî a mental health app designed to help you navigate stress, anxiety, depression, burnout, and loneliness in a way that feels natural and supportive. We believe mental well-being should be accessible to everyone, so we've made it easy for you to track your mood, explore guided exercises, and connect with a like-minded community‚Äîall at your own pace. Whether you're looking for simple self-care tips or deeper emotional support, Self Nurture is here to remind you that you're not alone. Your mental health matters, and we're here to help you take care of it, one step at a time.

The app offers a holistic approach by integrating self-care tools and professional resources, while emphasizing inclusivity for underserved groups like transgender individuals. By utilizing insights from user surveys, the app delivers personalized recommendations, empowering users to manage stress, anxiety, depression, sleep issues, and more.

Problem Statement

Mental health issues are increasingly prevalent, yet individuals face significant barriers such as accessibility, stigma, and a lack of personalized care. Existing mental health solutions often fail to address the specific needs of diverse demographics, leaving gaps in support, particularly for underserved groups like transgender individuals or those struggling with interconnected issues like fitness and mental health.

Solution Statement
